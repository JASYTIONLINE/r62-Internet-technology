# MedCertify Course Punchlist — CompTIA Network+

## Phase 1: Setup
- [x] Open **Welcome Letter** → confirm start & end dates:contentReference[oaicite:0]{index=0}.  
- [x] Log in to **MedCertify Dashboard**.  
- [x] Go to **Orientation Video** → watch in full:contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1}.  
- [x] Complete **Student Acknowledgment Form** (required before access):contentReference[oaicite:2]{index=2}.  
- [x] Bookmark **event calendar**: https://medcertify.com/event-calendar/ :contentReference[oaicite:3]{index=3}.  
- [x] Save **support email** (support@medcertify.com) and **academic coach contact info**:contentReference[oaicite:4]{index=4}.  

---

## Phase 2: Course Access
- [x] In Dashboard → click **Available Courses** tab:contentReference[oaicite:5]{index=5}.  
- [x] Select **CompTIA Network+ w/ Labs** course.  
- [x] Click **Enter** to move it into **Courses In Progress**:contentReference[oaicite:6]{index=6}.  

---

## Phase 3: Chapter Workflow (repeat for every chapter)
For each chapter listed in the syllabus:contentReference[oaicite:7]{index=7}:
1. [ ] Click course → click **Enter**.  
2. [ ] Open **Resources** → download/read workbook PDF:contentReference[oaicite:8]{index=8}.  
3. [ ] Press **Play** → watch training video(s) in full:contentReference[oaicite:9]{index=9}.  
4. [ ] Click **Quiz** → pass with **80%+** (retake until green check appears):contentReference[oaicite:10]{index=10}.  
5. [ ] Click **Labs** tab → choose lab title.  
   - [ ] Click **Launch** or **Start** depending on lab type:contentReference[oaicite:11]{index=11}.  
   - [ ] Complete lab exercises.  
6. [ ] Verify chapter checkmark turns green (dashboard progress).  

---

## Phase 4: Career Development (parallel track)
- [ ] Click **Career Development course**.  
- [ ] Watch assigned video(s).  
- [ ] Complete assignment in order (must do in sequence):contentReference[oaicite:12]{index=12}.  
- [ ] Repeat until all career development modules are done by program end date:contentReference[oaicite:13]{index=13}.  

---

## Phase 5: Mentoring & Support
- [ ] Check **event calendar** weekly.  
- [ ] Join **Live Mentoring Support session** (at least once per week recommended):contentReference[oaicite:14]{index=14}.  
- [ ] Ask questions, review material, or schedule 1-on-1 if needed:contentReference[oaicite:15]{index=15}.  

---

## Phase 6: Program Completion
- [ ] Finish all Network+ chapters, quizzes, and labs.  
- [ ] Confirm all **chapter checkmarks are green** in dashboard:contentReference[oaicite:16]{index=16}.  
- [ ] Finish all Career Development assignments.  
- [ ] Contact academic coach → complete **Exit Interview**:contentReference[oaicite:17]{index=17}.  
- [ ] Wait for **Certificate of Completion** (mailed in ~3 weeks; digital available on request):contentReference[oaicite:18]{index=18}.  
- [ ] Upload Certificate to **ArmyIgnitED** dashboard:contentReference[oaicite:19]{index=19}.  
- [ ] Confirm MedCertify reports completion after end date:contentReference[oaicite:20]{index=20}.  

---

## Phase 7: Exam Prep
- [ ] Launch **Exam Simulator** (available for 30 days post-completion):contentReference[oaicite:21]{index=21}.  
- [ ] Work through full simulator.  
- [ ] Within **14 days of finishing simulator**, click to **schedule exam registration**:contentReference[oaicite:22]{index=22}.  
- [ ] Confirm exam date is within **46 days of registration**:contentReference[oaicite:23]{index=23}.  

---

## Phase 8: Certification Exam
- [ ] Sit for official **CompTIA Network+ (N10-009) exam**.  
- [ ] Pass exam.  
- [ ] Notify academic coach (optional) and update resume with certification.  

---

# End State
You’ve worked the punchlist: all clicks done, certificate in hand, CompTIA exam passed. Success.
