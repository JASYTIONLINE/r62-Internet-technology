+ Common Security Terminology
  - Risk
    + The likelihood that something bad will happen
      - Qualitative vs. Quantitative Risk
        + Qualitative
          - Judgement-based
            + Steps to Qualitative Risk Analysis
              1. Identify Risk
              2. Classify Risk
              3. Control Risk
              4. Monitor Risk
        + Quantitative
          - Data-driven
  - Vulnerability
    + A specific weakness that affects a specific system
      - Weakness
        + https://cwe.mitre.org/
        + https://owasp.org/www-project-top-ten/
      - Vuln Databases
        + https://cve.mitre.org
        + https://nvd.nist.gov/
  - Threat
    + A specific threat-actor or threat-group that has the motive, means, and opportunity to exploit a vulnerability
      - Skids
      - Hacktivists
      - State-Sponsored
      - APT Groups
        + Lazarus Group
        + Fancy Bear
        + Sandworm
        + Cl0p
        + Royal Ransomware/Black Suit
