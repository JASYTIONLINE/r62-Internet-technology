## 2-2-1: Examining Authentication Factors

At the end of this episode, I will be able to:    

1. Identify and explain authentication concepts, given a scenario.    

Learner Objective: *Identify and explain authentication concepts, given a scenario*    

Description: In this episode, the learner will examine authentication, authentication factors and multifactor authentication or MFA.

--------  

* What is authentication?
	+ The process of validating a identity of a user or system.
* What are authentication factor?
	+ A distinct form of evidence or proof of identity.
* What authentication factors are there?
	+ Something you know
		- Username/password*
		- PIN
		- Secrets \(responses to questions\)
	+ Something you have
		- Smartcard
		- Key fob
		- Authenticator app
		- Registered/compliant device
	+ Something you are
		- Biometrics
		- DNA
	+ Somewhere you are
		- Location
		- Trusted network
	+ Something you do
		- Pattern recognition/arrangement
* What is multifactor authentication or MFA?
	+ The process of requiring more than one authentication factor
